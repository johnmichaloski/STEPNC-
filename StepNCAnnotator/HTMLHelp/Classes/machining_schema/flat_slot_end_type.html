ENTITY flat_slot_end_type

The end of the slot consists of a flat line with two arcs connecting the end to the sides of the slot. The radii of the two arcs are given. When traversing the slot along its center line from start to finish, the radius on the left side of the center line is corner_radius1, the radius on the right side of the center line is corner_radius2. The end point of course_of_travel is in the flat end of the slot.

Schema

machining_schema

Attributes

Attribute Type Description
corner_radius1 toleranced_length_measure Radius of the first arc.
corner_radius2 toleranced_length_measure Radius of the second arc.

Express Definition

ENTITY flat_slot_end_type 
SUBTYPE  OF (slot_end_type );
 corner_radius1 : toleranced_length_measure;
 corner_radius2 : toleranced_length_measure;
END_ENTITY;
