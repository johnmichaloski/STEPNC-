ENTITY approval

An approval is a confirmation of the quality of the product data that it is related to. Application protocols that use this resource construct shall specify the extent of the approval.

Schema

machining_schema

Attributes

Attribute Type Description
status approval_status the status of the approval in terms of whether or not the approval has been granted.
level label the type or level of approval in terms of its usage. This usage may be implied rather than explicit.

Express Definition

ENTITY approval ;
 status : approval_status;
 level : label;
END_ENTITY;
