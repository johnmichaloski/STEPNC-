ENTITY woodruff_slot_end_type

The end of slot shall be a radius tangent to the slot bottom and curved upward about an axis.

Schema

machining_schema

Attributes

Attribute Type Description
radius toleranced_length_measure Radius of the slot end type. The radius continues tangential from the bottom of the slot. It starts at the bottom of the slot where the course_of_travel ends. The radius does not necessarily equal to the depth of the slot.

Express Definition

ENTITY woodruff_slot_end_type 
SUBTYPE  OF (slot_end_type );
 radius : toleranced_length_measure;
END_ENTITY;
