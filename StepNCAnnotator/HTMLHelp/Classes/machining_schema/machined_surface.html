ENTITY machined_surface

The physical surface of machining_feature where the surface_texture_parameter will be applied.

Schema

machining_schema

Attributes

Attribute Type Description
its_machining_feature machining_feature The physical shape of the workpiece where the machined_surface belongs.
surface_element bottom_or_side The selection of the location of the machined_surface in reference to its_machining_feature

Express Definition

ENTITY machined_surface ;
 its_machining_feature : machining_feature;
 surface_element : bottom_or_side;
END_ENTITY;
