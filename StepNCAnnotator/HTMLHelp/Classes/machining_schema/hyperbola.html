ENTITY hyperbola

A hyperbola is a conic section defined by the lengths of the major and minor radii and the position (mid­point of the line joining two foci) and orientation of the curve.

Schema

machining_schema

Attributes

Attribute Type Description
semi_axis positive_length_measure : The length of the semi­axis of the hyperbola. This is positive and is half the minimum distance between the two branches of the hyperbola.
semi_imag_axis positive_length_measure The length of the semi­imaginary axis of the hyperbola which shall be positive.

Express Definition

ENTITY hyperbola 
SUBTYPE  OF (conic );
 semi_axis : positive_length_measure;
 semi_imag_axis : positive_length_measure;
END_ENTITY;
