ENTITY square_u_profile

A square U profile is a type of open profile that is bounded by three lines. One is the base line and has a defined length. The other two lines begin at the ends of the base line, and extend infinitely at any obtuse or acute angle that is equal to or larger than a right angle. The corners of the square U profile need not be blended by a radius.

Schema

machining_schema

Attributes

Attribute Type Description
width toleranced_length_measure he size of the base line for a square U profile.
first_radius toleranced_length_measure The radius shape blend between one side of the profile and the base.
first_angle plane_angle_measure The angle of the one side of the profile measured against the local z axis.
second_radius toleranced_length_measure The radius shape blend between the second side of the profile and the base.
second_angle plane_angle_measure The angle of the another side of the profile measured against the local z axis.
