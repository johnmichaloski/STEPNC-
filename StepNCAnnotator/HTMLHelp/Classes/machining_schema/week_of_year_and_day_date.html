ENTITY week_of_year_and_day_date

A week_of_year_and_date is a date which is identified by a day in a week of a year.

Schema

machining_schema

Attributes

Attribute Type Description
week_component week_in_year_number the week element of the date.
day_component day_in_week_number the day element of the date.

Express Definition

ENTITY week_of_year_and_day_date 
SUBTYPE  OF (date );
 week_component : week_in_year_number;
 day_component : OPTIONAL day_in_week_number;
END_ENTITY;
