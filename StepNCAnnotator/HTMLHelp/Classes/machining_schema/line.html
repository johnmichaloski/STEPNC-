ENTITY line

A line is an unbounded curve with constant tangent direction. A line is defined by a point and a direction. The positive direction of the line is in the direction of the dir vector.

Schema

machining_schema

Attributes

Attribute Type Description
pnt cartesian_point The location of the line.
dir vector The direction of the line; the magnitude and units of dir affect the parametrisation of the line.

Express Definition

ENTITY line 
SUBTYPE  OF (curve );
 pnt : cartesian_point;
 dir : vector;
END_ENTITY;
