ENTITY surface_of_revolution

A surface of revolution is the surface obtained by rotating a curve one complete revolution about an axis.

Schema

machining_schema

Attributes

Attribute Type Description
axis_position axis1_placement A point on the axis of revolution and the direction of the axis of revolution.

Express Definition

ENTITY surface_of_revolution 
SUBTYPE  OF (swept_surface );
 axis_position : axis1_placement;
END_ENTITY;
